FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Which course is best for a complete beginner?
For most students we recommend starting with CCNA (networking) or Python (programming) — both are beginner-friendly foundations. Our team gives free counselling to match a course to your goals.
Do you offer online classes?
Yes — every course is available as online, one-to-one, on-demand training, live with an instructor, taught in Urdu, Pashto, English or Hindi. You can join from anywhere in the world.
Will I get a certificate?
You receive a Corelinks course-completion certificate, and our courses prepare you for international certifications (Cisco, Microsoft, EC-Council, AWS and more).
How much do the courses cost?
Fees vary by course and mode (on-campus, online or corporate). Please contact us or WhatsApp for the current fee and any ongoing discounts.
Where are you located?
Our campus is on 1st Floor, Riaz Mart, Opposite Gul Haji Plaza, Main University Road, Peshawar. Online students are welcome from anywhere.
